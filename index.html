Second Semester



CashnTrack

CashnTrack is a budgeting program designed to simplify the process of managing your finances. With CashnTrack, you can easily track your income and expenses, helping you stay within your budget and achieve your financial goals. Simply input your income and expenses, and CashnTrack will automatically calculate your remaining balance also record your transactions ang categorize them.





Golden Car Rental Management System

Golden Car Rental business still use manual methods such as notebooks to record their daily transactions, rentals, and sales. The need for a more organized and more efficient system, this gives us motivation to develop Golden Car Rental Management System. The system will design to enable Golden Car Rental to record and track their cars, rentals, and sales electronically, enhancing the quality of management and services by the Golden Car Rental Management System.
